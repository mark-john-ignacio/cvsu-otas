Online Archiving Group BSIT 4-1 SY 2022-23 developed the Cavite State University - Online Archiving System as a solution to the university's unavailability of an online thesis archive; we proposed to create a Web-Based Thesis Archiving System using a Web database. With this project, CvSU-General Trias students will be able to access academic papers online from their university and keep academic papers in an online archive. We believe this will help researchers and students in the future find information that will help them with their work. It will also help student researchers get their works published and preserved. It will also greatly benefit the general discipline of knowledge-seeking and researching for the students of CvSU-General Trias, as it will conveniently give them timely and quick access to information and will serve as a medium for accessing quality-assured and trusted resources for the students at the university.
