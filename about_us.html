We, Group 7 of BSIT 3-1 SY 2021-22, developed Cavite State University - Online Thesis Archiving System as a solution to the unavailability of an online thesis archive of the university, we proposed to create a Web-Based Thesis Archiving System using a Web database. This project will give the CvSU-General Trias students availability to access academic papers online from their university and have an online archive to store and preserve academic papers. We believe that this will benefit future researchers and students in accessing valuable information for their work. It will also help student researchers to get their works published and preserved. It will also greatly benefit the general discipline of knowledge seeking and researching for the students of CvSU-General Trias as it will conveniently give them timely and quick access to information and will serve as a medium for accessing quality-assured and trusted resources for the students of the university.
